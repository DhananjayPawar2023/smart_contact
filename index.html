Remix Demo Steps

1. Open Remix IDE. 2. Deploy the smart contract in Remix VM (London). 3. Call addMovie("Inception", "A science fiction thriller"). 4. Call getMovie(1). 5. Rate using Account 1 with rateMovie(1, 5). 6. Switch to Account 2 and call rateMovie(1, 4). 7. Call getAverageRating(1). 8. Try rating again from the same account to show duplicate prevention.
